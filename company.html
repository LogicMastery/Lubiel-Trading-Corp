About Us

As early as the 50's started by our grandparents, with their consignation in the fish market of Navotas, the trading business has been a lifestyle for our family, bringing both foodstuff and non-perishable goods to our parents. Always committed to servicing our fellow Filipinos.

To our parents, handling their own trading, has been servicing the Bureau of Corrections, Tala Leprosarium, Heat Center of the Philippines, Veteran Memorial Medical Center and Philippine General Hospital as their clients since the 80's, and now onto the fourth generation, handling the requirements of Department of Social Welfare and Development amongst the others we cater to.

Our Services

Our mission is to fulfill the requirements of our parents, not limited to foodstuff but also, other non-perishable items our clients' needs, meeting their specifications and budget aiming to maintain a harmonious and enduring relationship. By keeping to the standards taught us, to uphold sincere and truthfull business practices, we envision the company to continue to provide for generations to come.
